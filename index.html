About us

Towering over Edinburgh’s skyline, our spectacular Ferris Wheel is a thrill-packed attraction that draws crowds from near and far. A must-visit highlight of the city at Christmas, it has become a focal point of excitement, offering unrivaled views and an unforgettable experience. With breathtaking heights, dazzling lights, or just the sheer fun, this iconic wheel is a showstopper that keeps visitors coming back year after year every Christmas .

But why stop at Christmas? Our versatile Ferris Wheel transforms with the seasons, making it the perfect centrepiece for summer festivals, shopping centres, and special events.

Key features

GONDOLAS AND PASSENGER EXPERIENCE

  • 36 Gondolas with Automatic Loading: Equipped with an automatic loading system for faster boarding and reduced wait times.
  • Climate-Controlled Comfort: Each gondola features air conditioning and heating, ensuring passenger comfort in all climates.
  • Capacity: Each gondola holds 6 passengers, with the entire wheel capable of handling up to 1,800 passengers per hour.
  • Onboard Entertainment: Gondolas are fitted with a music system, allowing custom audio messages or music for an engaging passenger experience.

LIGHTING AND VISUAL APPEALE

  • The wheel is illuminated by over 100,000 LED lights, offering stunning, customizable lighting displays for both daytime and nighttime operations.

TRANSPORT AND SETUP

  • Fully Transportable Design: The wheel can be transported using 9 articulated trailers, making it highly mobile and ideal for temporary installations.
  • Quick Setup: The structure can be assembled in just 2 days, minimizing downtime and maximizing operational efficiency.
